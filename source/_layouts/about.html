About Me (CV)

I'm a Researcher in Borealis AI, aiming at publishing in top conferences in Machine Learning (ICLR/ICML/NeurIPS) and Natural Language Processing (ACL/NAACL/EMNLP) and developing products enabled by the cutting-edge AI technology.

Before joining Borealis AI, I was a master student of Department of Computing Science at University of Alberta. I worked at the Database Group where I was advised by Prof. Denilson Barbosa. I'm also an active contributor of DBpedia. I've participated in GSoC 2016 as a student (Certificate) and GSoC 2017 and 2018 as a student mentor (Certificate) for DBpedia.

I got my B.S. degree in the School of Information and Communication Engineering of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications(BUPT). I finished my B.S. graduation thesis in CS department in Tsinghua university in June 2015. I really appreciate my thesis advisor Prof. Jie Tang for his guidance and help in that year.
